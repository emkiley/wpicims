Mission

The primary aim of the Center is to build partnerships between the university and the industrial sponsor that benefit the sponsors, the students and the faculty.

In addition to helping companies address their needs for mathematical solutions and enhance their technological competitiveness, the Center offers undergraduates and graduate students at WPI the opportunity to gain real-world experience in the corporate world that can make them more competitive in today's job market.

Through the partnerships developed with the industry, the faculty associated to the Center gain valuable experience that enriches their teaching and research.
