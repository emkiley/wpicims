Seminar Banquet


A Seminar Gala Dinner will be held on Monday, March 7, 2011 at 19:30 in the Waisenhaus Ristorante, the Thun representative of Bindella, the famous Swiss restaurant chain. Below is the view of Waisenhaus from Bälliz, the main street of downtown Thun.

In the framework of special arrangements for the seminar participants, Waisenhaus estimates the cost of the dinner to be about 60 CHF per person. The restaurant kindly asks its future guests to indicate, through the Seminar Registration form, any preferences regarding the meal for the dinner (vegetarian, etc.) - this would help them plan accordingly.
