The Problem

Many insurance companies provide discounts for multiple cars covered under the same policy. Do these multiple car discount policies incur less loss than non-multiple car policies?

Areas of Application

Basic Statistics including
  • Mean and median
  • Box & Whisker Plots
  • Histograms
  • Hypothesis tests: Wilcoxon Rank-Sum Test & Sign Test

  • Instructions for making a box & whisker plots
  • Solutions and suggestions
