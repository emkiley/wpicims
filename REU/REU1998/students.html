Talks

  • My First Year at Microsoft (Stanislav Oks, Microsoft, WPI Graduate 1997)
    Stanislav described the Microsoft working environment, told how a mathematician found his way there and what keeps him there. Why is training in mathematics much more useful than training in computer science.
  • Mathematics in Finance (Keith Hartt, Fidelity Investment)
    What it's like to be a mathematician in an industry full of business people. You have to have confidence in yourself and your mathematics!
